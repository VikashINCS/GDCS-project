Google Assistance

Weather Updates: Stay informed about the weather through Google Assistant. This can help you plan your activities, reduce unnecessary energy consumption, and adapt to changing weather conditions.

Eco-friendly Tips and Information: Google Assistant can provide information and tips on living a more eco-friendly lifestyle. You can ask for advice on reducing waste, conserving energy, or using sustainable products.

Environmental News Updates: Stay informed about climate-related news by asking Google Assistant for updates.

Google Map

  • Google Maps can provide information on the locations of electric vehicle charging stations.
  • Promote community cleanup events by marking locations on Google Maps.
  • Real-time traffic data in Google Maps can help users avoid congested routes. Smoother traffic flow reduces fuel consumption and emissions associated with idling in traffic.
